1.  Capitalize one-character word
    Ex. 'x' -> 'X'
2.  Capitalize first letter of a single word
    Ex. 'terminator' -> 'Terminator'
3.  Capitalize first letter of multiple word string
    Ex. 'terminator genisys' -> 'Terminator Genisys'
4.  Does not capitalize designated words (prepositions, conjuctions, etc)
    Ex. 'the lord of the rings' -> 'the Lord of the Rings'
5.  Capitalizes designated words if they are the first word
    Ex. 'the lord of the rings' -> 'The Lord of the Rings'
6.  Handles non-letter characters
    Ex. '101 dalmations' -> '101 Dalmations'
7.  Manages all uppercase entries
    Ex. 'TERMINATOR' -> 'Terminator'
8.  Manages mixed case entries
    Ex. 'TeRmiNaToR' -> 'Terminator'
9.  Manages unique case entries
    Ex. 'McDuff' -> 'McDuff'
10. Correctly capitalize hyphenated words
    Ex. "x-files" -> "X-Files"
